Hi, I'm James Lutley, a Product Designer and Ruby on Rails developer living in Da Nang, Vietnam.

I’m a passionate, creative Product Designer, Front-end developer, and budding Ruby on Rails developer. I equally love designing and coding, and happily jump between disciplines. I’m also a Vim and tmux enthusiast and type in Dvorak. A productivity geek, I like to automate most mundane tasks. I work remotely on a range of projects, and I’m always open to hearing about interesting new opportunities.

When I’m not busy creating things for the web, I enjoy travelling, photography, listening to music, long walks on the beach, playing guitar, and snowboarding. Originally from the UK, I’ve lived in Australia, did an amazing snowboarding season in New Zealand, travelled around much of Southeast Asia, and now live in Da Nang, Vietnam. In a previous life I used to be a lawyer, and I designed and built a professional music recording studio.
